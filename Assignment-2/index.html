The Invisible Life of Addie LaRue

By V.E. Schwab

About the Book

Adeline "Addie" LaRue from 1714 France to today, tries to make a deal with the devil to gain eternal life but at the same time, she will be forgotten by all people she comes across. Her life is turned upside down 300 years later when she meets a guy who knows her name. A haunting, unforgettable tale about existence, love, and making an impression on the planet.
